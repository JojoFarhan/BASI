About BASI

Bangladesh Association for Social Improvement (BASI) is a registered non-profit development organization dedicated to supporting the socio-economic advancement of underprivileged communities across Bangladesh.

Established in 1997 under the Society Act XXI of 1860 (Reg. No. S-1883/59), BASI has been actively involved in social research, rehabilitation, and development consulting.

With extensive experience in involuntary resettlement, rehabilitation, and socio-economic surveys, BASI has contributed to major infrastructure and development projects funded by the Asian Development Bank (ADB), World Bank, and JICA.
